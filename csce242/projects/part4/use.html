Since this app is a starter app it hasn't reached its max potential but for now here are some things you can do:

  • Log in and create an account
  • Add multiple movies to your playlist through the add playlist link in the profile page
  • Find the newest news on movies that fellow users have made through the blog page
  • Discover new movies by going to the discover tab that searches google for you
  • Go to settings if you need more assistance or to change up your site
  • Home is the place that lists your friends and your playlist so you have easy access to it all
